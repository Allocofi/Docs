With the widespread adoption of stablecoins, global investors can now seamlessly cross the boundary between traditional finance and blockchain, using tokens to freely access stocks, bonds, and commodities. However, the explosion of available assets has brought fragmented allocations and complex cross-platform operations, leaving everyday investors facing a new kind of paralysis. To solve this challenge, Alloco draws on the wisdom of traditional ETFs to build a comprehensive **onchain ETF Token (ETFT) infrastructure**. By repackaging scattered assets into transparent, dynamically valued, and freely tradable onchain products, Alloco transforms top hedge fund strategies, KOL portfolio insights, and congressional trading disclosures into single-click financial instruments—quietly turning the page for internet-native markets.

## What is On-chain ETFTs?

An **Onchain ETF Token (ETFT)** is a tokenized financial instrument that packages a diversified portfolio of assets, systematic trading strategies, or market narratives into a single, fully transparent, and programmatically rebalanced on-chain product. Powered by smart contract infrastructure, an ETFT features defined rules, real-time Net Asset Value (NAV) tracking, and continuous liquidity, allowing investors to gain friction-free, single-token exposure to complex global investment themes without the operational overhead of managing individual assets across multiple platforms.
