Before you access, subscribe to, purchase, hold, or redeem any Alloco product, you must confirm that you meet the eligibility requirements that apply to you. Access to Alloco products requires meeting criteria across four dimensions: basic legal capacity, investor classification, identity verification, and jurisdictional compliance. Some of these requirements apply to the platform as a whole; others are specific to individual products. Read this page carefully, and review the Product Documents for any product you intend to access.

## Restricted Jurisdictions

You may not use the Services if doing so would violate any applicable sanctions, securities laws, financial-services laws, AML laws, or other legal restrictions in your jurisdiction or in any jurisdiction relevant to your activities. This includes jurisdictions where the offer or sale of financial products like those available on Alloco is prohibited or requires local registration, licensing, or approval that has not been obtained.

Alloco uses a combination of the following controls to enforce jurisdictional restrictions:

- **IP geolocation** — to identify and block connections originating from restricted jurisdictions
- **Identity verification** — to confirm your country of residence and citizenship
- **Wallet screening** — to identify wallets associated with restricted regions or sanctioned entities
- **Blockchain analytics** — to detect patterns consistent with attempts to circumvent access controls

<Warning>
  You must not attempt to circumvent a jurisdictional, investor-classification, sanctions, wallet, or access restriction, including through a virtual private network, proxy, false statement, nominee, or third party. Any such attempt may result in immediate termination of your access and may expose you to legal liability.
</Warning>
