Alloco is financial product issuance and access infrastructure built for internet-native markets. The platform converts market narratives, professional insights, investment themes, and risk exposures into On-chain ETP products with clear rules, transparent valuation, onchain tradability, and full risk disclosure — giving investors around the world access to Wall Street hedge funds, leading market voices, congressional trading disclosures, and global investment themes.

## What is On-chain ETPs?

The internet changed how information moves, how people communicate, and how payments flow. But capital markets never truly became internet-native. Financial products have remained locked inside brokerage accounts, geographic borders, legacy infrastructure, and closed distribution networks. For decades, the power to create and distribute investable products has been concentrated in the hands of a few large financial institutions. Alloco was built to change that.

At its core, Alloco is the **Onchain ETP OS** — a product layer that helps institutions, platforms, and asset issuers create and distribute financial products as easily as internet companies launch applications:

- **Financial product infrastructure, not just tokenization.** Tokenization turns assets into digital units. Alloco turns assets into financial products. A real product needs more than a token — it needs structure, rules, NAV, custody, liquidity, redemption, compliance, and distribution.
- **Clear rules, visible pricing.** Every product defines its eligible assets, valuation methodology, rebalancing process, liquidity path, and redemption constraints. Nothing is hidden.
- **End-to-end product lifecycle support.** From the initial investment thesis through structuring, NAV calculation, access routing, and ongoing disclosure, Alloco engineers the full product reality so issuers can focus on the idea.
- **Open and composable.** Products are accessible through DEX, RFQ, wallet, exchange, aggregator, or other distribution channels — globally, 24/7.
