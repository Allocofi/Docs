## Step 1 — Submit Redemption Request

Select the ETP you want to redeem and enter the amount of tokens you wish to redeem.

Alloco will generate a redemption order containing:

- Selected ETP product
- Redemption amount
- Current estimated NAV
- Expected settlement details
- Applicable redemption fees

After reviewing the order details, confirm the redemption request from your wallet.

**What happens next?**

Your ETP tokens are transferred to the redemption process and locked until settlement is completed.

## Step 2 — Redemption Processing

Once your redemption request is submitted, Alloco begins the settlement process.

Your order moves through several stages:

### 1. Redemption Submitted

Your redemption request has been successfully created.

### 2. ETP Tokens Received

The requested ETP tokens have been verified and received.

### 3. NAV Calculation

The latest verified NAV is used to determine the redemption value.

### 4. Basket Settlement

The underlying assets are processed according to the ETP redemption rules.

### 5. Settlement Confirmed

The redemption amount is finalized and prepared for distribution.

### 6. USDC Transfer

The corresponding USDC amount is sent back to your wallet.

## Step 3 — Redeemed Successfully

After settlement is completed, your redemption details will be displayed.

You will receive:

- Redemption NAV
- Redeemed ETP units
- Settlement amount
- Fees applied
- Settlement timestamp

Your ETP tokens are burned after redemption, ensuring that outstanding supply remains aligned with the underlying assets.

## Transparent Redemption

Every redemption request is tracked from submission to settlement.

Alloco provides visibility into:

- Redemption status
- NAV calculation
- Token burn records
- Settlement history
- Final payout amount

The redemption process ensures that every ETP unit remains backed by transparent asset value.
