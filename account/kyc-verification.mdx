Accessing certain Alloco ETP products requires identity verification (KYC/KYB). This process confirms your eligibility, investor classification, and compliance with anti-money laundering (AML) and sanctions requirements. Verification is conducted by Alloco or its licensed third-party providers, and the information you submit is handled in accordance with the [Alloco Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy-policy).

## What Information Is Required

The specific documents and details requested will depend on the product you are trying to access and your jurisdiction. You may be asked to provide some or all of the following.

### Personal Information

- Full legal name
- Date and place of birth
- Nationality
- Residential address
- Government-issued identification documents (such as a passport, national ID card, or driver's licence)
- Photographs or identity verification images (for liveness or document verification checks)

### Financial and Compliance Information

- Tax identification information (e.g., TIN, SSN, or equivalent)
- Source of funds or source of wealth
- Accredited, professional, or qualified investor status
- Beneficial ownership and control information (required for entity accounts — see [Business/Entity Accounts](#businessentity-accounts) below)

## The Verification Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet and log in">
    Start from [app.allocofi.com](https://app.allocofi.com) after connecting your digital wallet and completing basic account setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Begin the KYC flow">
    Navigate to the identity verification section of your account dashboard. You may be redirected to a third-party verification provider to complete document upload and identity checks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit required documents">
    Upload your identification documents and any additional compliance information requested. Ensure documents are valid, clearly legible, and not expired.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for verification results">
    Verification may be completed in minutes or may take up to a few business days depending on the complexity of your application and any additional review required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your account for confirmation">
    Once verified, log in to your account to see which products you are eligible to access. You will receive a notification when your verification status changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Successful verification does not automatically create a right to access any specific product. Products may have additional eligibility requirements, such as accredited investor status or jurisdiction-specific restrictions, beyond the standard KYC process.
</Note>

## Wallet and Blockchain Screening

In addition to verifying your identity, Alloco conducts ongoing due diligence at the wallet and transaction level. This includes:

- **Wallet address screening** — your connected wallet address is checked against international sanctions lists and known high-risk addresses.
- **Blockchain analytics** — Alloco uses blockchain intelligence tools to assess the origin and history of funds associated with your wallet.
- **Ongoing transaction monitoring** — activity on your account is monitored on a continuous basis for patterns that may indicate suspicious or prohibited conduct.

These screening measures are a regulatory requirement and apply to all users, regardless of jurisdiction.

## What Alloco May Do with Your Information

After you submit your verification information, Alloco or its service providers may take the following actions as part of the compliance process:

- Verify the information you provide against public and private databases
- Delay a transaction while conducting an enhanced review
- Impose transaction limits appropriate to your verified status
- Reject an application that does not meet eligibility requirements
- Suspend access to the platform or specific products if compliance requirements are not met or are no longer satisfied
- Report information to competent authorities where required or permitted by applicable law

## Privacy and Data Security

Your identity information is collected, processed, and stored in accordance with the [Alloco Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy-policy). Alloco uses administrative, technical, and organisational safeguards to protect the information you submit during verification.

Identity verification may be conducted by a third-party provider. In that case, Alloco may receive verification results, risk indicators, or confirmation that the process was completed — rather than retaining a full copy of your documents directly.

For questions about how your data is handled, contact [support@allocofi.com](mailto:support@allocofi.com).

## Business/Entity Accounts

If you are accessing Alloco as a company, fund, or other legal entity, you will be required to complete KYB (Know Your Business) verification in addition to — or instead of — individual KYC. KYB verification may include:

- Company registration documents (e.g., certificate of incorporation, memorandum and articles of association)
- Beneficial ownership information, including details of all individuals with significant ownership or control
- Business address and principal contact information
- Description of the nature and purpose of the business
- Regulatory licences or authorisations, where applicable
- Source of funds or source of wealth at the entity level
- Supporting documentation for directors, authorised signatories, and ultimate beneficial owners

Entity accounts may face additional review requirements or longer processing times. Contact [support@allocofi.com](mailto:support@allocofi.com) if you need assistance with entity onboarding.
