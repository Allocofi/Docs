## What is Alloco?

Alloco is not just tokenization. Tokenization turns assets into digital units. Alloco turns assets into **financial products**.

A real product needs more than a token — it needs structure, rules, NAV, custody, liquidity, redemption, compliance, and distribution. Alloco's Onchain ETP OS is a product layer that helps institutions, platforms, and asset issuers create and distribute financial products as easily as internet companies launch applications.
