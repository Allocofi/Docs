# Theme Selection

Every ETP begins with an investment thesis.

Creators define:

- Market opportunity
- Target assets
- Investment objective
- Portfolio methodology

Examples:

- Artificial Intelligence Infrastructure
- Robotics
- Semiconductor Supply Chain
- Digital Assets
- Commodities
- Private Markets

---

# Product Configuration

Creators configure:

## Underlying Assets

Supported assets may include:

- Public equities
- ETFs
- Commodities
- Digital assets
- Alternative assets

## Portfolio Rules

Allocation methodologies may include:

- Fixed allocation
- Market-cap weighted
- Factor-based
- Quantitative strategies
- Custom methodologies

## Risk Parameters

Creators define:

- Position limits
- Rebalancing frequency
- Exposure limits
- Liquidity requirements

---

# ETP Factory

ETP Factory creates the product infrastructure.

It manages:

- Product deployment
- Token generation
- Smart contract configuration
- Asset accounting
- Supply management

Each ETP receives:

- Product identifier
- Token contract
- NAV framework
- Documentation package
