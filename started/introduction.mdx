With the widespread adoption of stablecoins, global investors can now seamlessly cross the boundary between traditional finance and blockchain, using tokens to freely access stocks, bonds, and commodities. However, the explosion of available assets has brought fragmented allocations and complex cross-platform operations, leaving everyday investors facing a new kind of paralysis. To solve this challenge, Alloco draws on the wisdom of traditional ETFs to build a comprehensive onchain ETF Token (ETFT) infrastructure. By repackaging scattered assets into transparent, dynamically valued, and freely tradable on-chain products, Alloco transforms top hedge fund strategies, KOL portfolio insights, and congressional trading disclosures into single-click financial instruments—quietly turning the page for internet-native markets.

## What is On-chain ETFTs?

Imagine you believe the next wave of AI will be powered by data centers, energy, semiconductors and cooling infrastructure.

To invest in that idea, you would normally need to research multiple assets, open the right accounts, manage each position and rebalance the portfolio yourself.

ETFTs made this easier by packaging a basket of assets into one tradable product.

An On-chain ETFT brings the same idea onchain.

**It packages a portfolio, strategy or investment theme into a single tokenized product, with defined rules and a transparent net asset value.**

Instead of managing every asset individually, you can access the entire idea through one product.

**Think of it as an ETF, built for onchain markets.**
