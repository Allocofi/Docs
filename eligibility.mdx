Before you access, subscribe to, purchase, hold, or redeem any Alloco product, you must confirm that you meet the eligibility requirements that apply to you. Access to Alloco products requires meeting criteria across four dimensions: basic legal capacity, investor classification, identity verification, and jurisdictional compliance. Some of these requirements apply to the platform as a whole; others are specific to individual products. Read this page carefully, and review the Product Documents for any product you intend to access.

## Basic Requirements

You may only use the Alloco platform and access its products if you satisfy all of the following:

- You are at least **18 years old** (or the age of legal majority in your jurisdiction, whichever is higher).
- You have the **legal capacity** to enter into a binding agreement under the laws of your jurisdiction.
- Your use of the Services is **lawful in your jurisdiction** — it does not violate any applicable law, regulation, or rule.
- You are **not subject to sanctions** or asset-freeze restrictions imposed by any government, regulatory authority, or international body.
- You are **not acting on behalf of a prohibited person** — including any person or entity that would itself be ineligible to access the Services.
- You satisfy any applicable **KYC, AML, investor classification, and product eligibility requirements** as determined by Alloco or the relevant product issuer.

## Investor Classification

Certain Alloco products are not available to the general public. Depending on the product, access may be limited to one or more of the following investor categories:

- **Accredited investors** — as defined under applicable securities law in your jurisdiction
- **Professional investors** — persons or entities with sufficient experience, knowledge, and expertise to make their own investment decisions
- **Qualified investors** — as defined under the applicable fund or regulatory framework
- **Eligible counterparties** — institutional-grade entities acting in a wholesale capacity
- **Institutional investors** — banks, asset managers, insurance companies, pension funds, and similar entities
- **Persons located in specified jurisdictions** — where the product has been structured or approved for distribution

You are responsible for accurately determining and representing your investor classification before accessing any product.

<Note>
  Access to the general website does not mean that a particular ETP is offered or available to you. Each product may have its own eligibility requirements defined in the Product Documents.
</Note>

## Identity Verification

Alloco and its service providers conduct Know Your Customer (KYC) and Know Your Business (KYB) verification before granting product access. To complete verification, you must:

- **Provide accurate identity information** — including name, address, date of birth, government-issued identification, and any other information required by the verification process.
- **Consent to database checks** — Alloco or its service providers may verify your identity and status against public and private databases, sanctions lists, and politically exposed persons (PEP) registries.
- **Submit to wallet and blockchain screening** — your connected wallet address and onchain transaction history may be screened for compliance purposes, including checks against flagged addresses and sanctioned entities.
- **Understand that verification is not a guarantee** — successfully completing identity verification does not automatically grant you access to any specific product. Product-level eligibility requirements apply separately.

All information you provide must be complete, accurate, and current. You must promptly update your information if it changes.

## Restricted Jurisdictions

You may not use the Services if doing so would violate any applicable sanctions, securities laws, financial-services laws, AML laws, or other legal restrictions in your jurisdiction or in any jurisdiction relevant to your activities. This includes jurisdictions where the offer or sale of financial products like those available on Alloco is prohibited or requires local registration, licensing, or approval that has not been obtained.

Alloco uses a combination of the following controls to enforce jurisdictional restrictions:

- **IP geolocation** — to identify and block connections originating from restricted jurisdictions
- **Identity verification** — to confirm your country of residence and citizenship
- **Wallet screening** — to identify wallets associated with restricted regions or sanctioned entities
- **Blockchain analytics** — to detect patterns consistent with attempts to circumvent access controls

<Warning>
  You must not attempt to circumvent a jurisdictional, investor-classification, sanctions, wallet, or access restriction, including through a virtual private network, proxy, false statement, nominee, or third party. Any such attempt may result in immediate termination of your access and may expose you to legal liability.
</Warning>
