Alloco is committed to protecting your privacy. This page summarises how Alloco Limited and its applicable affiliates collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you access or use the platform — including allocofi.com, app.allocofi.com, and all related interfaces, tools, and services.

<Note>
  The full Privacy Policy is available at [allocofi.com/privacy-policy](https://allocofi.com/privacy-policy). This page is a summary for reference. The full policy controls in all cases.
</Note>

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Services, Alloco and its service providers automatically collect technical and usage information, including:

- IP address, browser type, device type, and operating system
- Pages viewed, features accessed, links clicked, and session duration
- Referring and exit pages, date and time of access, and error reports
- Security and authentication events

### Wallet and Blockchain Data

When you connect a wallet or interact with the platform on-chain, Alloco may collect or process:

- Your public wallet address and blockchain network
- Token and ETP Unit balances
- Transaction hashes and transaction history
- Smart contract interactions, approvals, and signatures
- Asset transfers, timestamps, and associated public addresses
- Risk indicators associated with your wallet or transactions

<Note>
  Alloco does not receive or store your private keys or seed phrases. Never provide these to Alloco or anyone claiming to represent Alloco.
</Note>

## Who Information May Be Shared With

Alloco does not sell your personal information for monetary consideration. It may disclose your information to the following categories of recipients:

| Recipient Category | Why |
|---|---|
| ETP issuers and product entities | To process subscriptions/redemptions, maintain investor registers, and satisfy regulatory requirements for a specific product |
| Service providers | Cloud hosting, KYC/AML screening, blockchain analytics, identity verification, customer support, legal, audit, and compliance services |
| Financial and transaction counterparties | Brokers, custodians, banks, payment providers, settlement providers, market makers, and liquidity providers involved in executing or settling your transactions |
| Affiliates and business partners | Where reasonably necessary to provide, operate, develop, or support the Services |
| Legal and regulatory authorities | Where required or permitted by law — including regulators, tax authorities, courts, law enforcement, and sanctions authorities |
| Corporate transaction parties | In connection with a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or asset sale, subject to applicable law |

## Data Retention

Alloco retains your personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal and regulatory requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. The applicable retention period depends on the nature of the information, the purpose for which it was collected, and applicable law.

Compliance and financial transaction records may be retained for several years after your relationship with Alloco ends, as required by AML, tax, and financial recordkeeping regulations.

<Note>
  Information recorded on a public blockchain — such as your wallet address, transaction hashes, and on-chain activity — may remain permanently available and cannot be deleted or altered by Alloco.
</Note>
