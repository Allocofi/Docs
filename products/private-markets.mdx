## PE&VC Opportunities

Alloco curates access to private equity and venture capital opportunities at various stages of the company growth lifecycle — from early venture through to growth equity and pre-IPO. These opportunities are structured as onchain products with defined terms, NAV methodology, and custody arrangements, giving you the same disclosure standards and structural clarity you expect from any other Alloco product. Access may be limited by eligibility requirements and jurisdiction. Check each product page for the specific terms and minimum participation amounts.

## Manager-Led Onchain Products

Qualified fund managers and investment professionals can launch independently branded onchain investment products through Alloco's issuance infrastructure. This pathway is designed for managers who want the reach and accessibility of onchain distribution without sacrificing the product quality, compliance standards, or branding that define their offering.

Launching a manager-led product through Alloco includes:

- **Full product structuring and blueprint design** — defining eligible assets, strategy rules, fees, and rebalancing processes
- **NAV engine integration** — connecting your strategy's valuation logic to Alloco's three-layer NAV system for Official NAV, iNAV, and Risk NAV
- **Liquidity and trading setup** — configuring DEX, RFQ, and primary create/redeem flows to match your product's liquidity profile
- **Compliance and disclosure infrastructure** — prospectus, risk disclosures, NAV methodology, custody overview, and ongoing reporting
- **Distribution through Alloco's access network** — reaching investors through Alloco's platform, DEX integrations, and access channels
