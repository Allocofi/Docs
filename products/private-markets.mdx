Alloco's ETP fee structure is designed to align with long-term investors while maintaining transparent and predictable costs.

The total cost of holding an ETP consists of:

- Minting fees
- Redemption fees
- Management fees
- Other operational expenses

# 1. Mint Fee

## No Mint Fee

Alloco does not charge any fee when users mint new ETP units.

Users receive ETP units based on the current NAV per unit:

$$
Minted\ Units=
\frac{Deposit}
{NAV_{per\ Unit}}
$$

This structure allows investors to enter an ETP at the same underlying asset value without additional issuance costs.

# 2. Redemption Fee

Redemption fees are designed to encourage long-term holding and protect ETP liquidity.

The redemption fee may be reduced or waived depending on the holding period.

$$
Redemption\ Fee=f(Holding\ Period)
$$

Where:

| Component | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Holding Period | Duration that ETP units are held before redemption |
| Redemption Fee | Fee applied when redeeming ETP units |

## Holding Period Example

| Holding Period | Redemption Fee |
| --- | --- |
| Short-term holding | Applicable fee |
| Medium-term holding | Reduced fee |
| Long-term holding | Potential fee waiver |

The final redemption amount is calculated as:

$$
USDC_{Received}
=
Units
\times
NAV_{Unit}
\times
(1-Redemption\ Fee)
$$

# 3. Management Fee

Alloco charges an annual management fee based on the ETP assets under management.

The management fee range:

$$
\boxed{
0.5\%-2.0\% \ per\ year
}
$$

The management fee is accrued over time:

$$
Management\ Fee_t
=
NAV_t
\times
Annual\ Fee
\times
\frac{Days}{365}
$$

Where:

| Component | Description |
| --- | --- |
| NAV | Net asset value of the ETP |
| Annual Fee | Annual management fee rate |
| Days | Number of days elapsed |

## Example

Assume:

- ETP NAV: \$10,000,000
- Management fee: 1% annually
- Holding period: 30 days

The accrued fee:

$$
10,000,000
\times
1\%
\times
\frac{30}{365}
$$

$$
=
8,219.18
$$

The fee is reflected in NAV calculation.

# 4. Other Expenses

Certain operational expenses may be reflected in the ETP NAV.

These may include:

| Expense Category | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Custody Costs | Fees charged by qualified custodians |
| Brokerage Costs | Execution and settlement costs |
| Trading Costs | Market execution related expenses |
| Network Fees | Blockchain transaction costs |
| Administration Costs | Fund administration and reporting expenses |

# 5. Total Cost Impact

The effective cost of an ETP can be summarized as:

$$
Total\ Cost
=
Management\ Fee
+
Redemption\ Fee
+
Operational\ Expenses
$$

Minting does not incur additional issuance fees:

$$
Mint\ Fee=0
$$

# Transparent Fee Model

All fees are disclosed and reflected through the ETP NAV calculation.

Investors can verify:

- Current NAV
- Accrued management fees
- Redemption terms
- Operational expenses

This ensures that every ETP unit represents a transparent and verifiable ownership interest in the underlying assets.
