## Product Types

Leveraging our native ETFT infrastructure, Alloco develops three specialized categories of financial products tailored to dynamic market demand. Products of this nature were historically the exclusive domain of traditional asset management giants like BlackRock and State Street. Through Alloco’s smart contract architecture, these structured strategies are now fully programmable, transparent, and accessible directly on-chain.

| Product Type | Description | Representative Examples |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Key Opinion Leader (KOL) ETFT** | Captures market sentiment by tracking public portfolios, high-conviction insights, and regulatory disclosures of influential figures and policymakers. | Pelosi’s Portfolio, Trump’s Portfolio, X’s KOL Concept |
| **Hedge Fund Copy ETFT** | Systematically replicates the public filings, long-term holdings, and top-tier strategies of premier institutional asset managers. | Duan Yongping ETFT (DYP), Coatue ETFT |
| **Thematic ETFT** | Aggregates targeted baskets of assets to deliver single-token exposure to emerging tech shifts, sector narratives, and macro megatrends. | Robotics ETFT (RBI) |

## Product Objectives

Every product on Alloco is designed to meet a consistent set of structural objectives. These objectives define how a product is built, priced, traded, and disclosed.

| Objective | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Understandable** | Clear narrative, underlying asset or strategy description, target exposure and risk boundary. |
| **Valuable** | Defined NAV methodology, data sources, update frequency and exception-handling rules. |
| **Tradable** | Accessible through DEX, RFQ, wallet, exchange, aggregator or other distribution channel. |
| **Redeemable** | Eligible users, APs, MMs or institutions may create/redeem through primary market processes. |
| **Protected** | Risk-state logic, NAV staleness checks, premium/discount monitoring, redemption limits and suspension mechanisms. |
| **Disclosed** | Users can review terms, fees, risks, NAV, holdings, strategy logic, custody information and reports. |

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discover an ETFT">
    Explore products built around people, strategies, themes, or innovative indices.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Understand the Exposure">
    Review the underlying assets, strategy rules, performance, fees, and key risks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Eligibility">
    Connect a wallet and complete the required eligibility and compliance checks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invest in One Product">
    Subscribe using supported assets and receive ETFT units representing the selected exposure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track and Trade">
    Monitor NAV, portfolio holdings, performance, and market price in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Redeem When Ready">
    Redeem ETFT units based on the applicable NAV and receive the supported settlement asset.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Product names containing terms such as "Stable," "Yield," "Hedge," "Arbitrage," "Income," or "Protection" do not imply principal protection, guaranteed yield, no risk or fixed return.
</Note>
