Hedge Fund ETPs bring proven institutional strategies onchain. Instead of navigating complex fund access requirements — minimum commitments, lock-up periods, opaque reporting — you can gain exposure to professional fund strategies through a structured, transparent ETP with clear rules, defined NAV, and accessible trading. Each product is built to reflect the intent of the underlying strategy while maintaining the disclosure standards Alloco requires across all products.

## What is a Hedge Fund ETP?

Each Hedge Fund ETP is built around a specific strategy or approach from a qualified fund manager or professional investment team. The underlying strategy is modeled into an ETP structure with defined eligible assets, a fee schedule, a rebalancing process, and redemption terms. Rather than investing directly into a fund — with all the paperwork, access barriers, and reporting delays that entails — you access the same strategic exposure through an onchain product with real-time NAV reference, clear custody arrangements, and defined redemption rights.

## Product Structure

Hedge Fund ETPs may reference a wide range of professional strategies. Common approaches include:

- **Market-neutral strategies** — spot-perp arbitrage, basis, and funding rate capture
- **Long/short equity strategies** — taking directional or relative-value positions across public equities
- **Macro or multi-asset strategies** — positioning across rates, FX, commodities, and broader market cycles
- **Quantitative rule-based approaches** — systematic models driving allocation and rebalancing decisions

The underlying exposure may be crypto-native or include TradFi-linked assets accessed through broker API, custody account, swap, note, or SPV structure — giving the product flexibility to reflect a broad range of institutional strategies.
