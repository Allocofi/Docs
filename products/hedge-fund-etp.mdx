## Step 1 — Confirm Your Deposit

### Submit your mint request

Select the ETP you want to create and enter your deposit amount.

Alloco will generate a subscription order containing:

- Selected ETP product
- Deposit asset (e.g. USDC)
- Deposit amount
- Network information
- Mint contract address
- Estimated transaction fee

After reviewing the order details, confirm the transaction from your wallet.

**What happens next?**

Your USDC deposit is received by the Alloco minting process and a subscription order is created.

---

# Step 2 — Order Processing

### Alloco executes the underlying assets

After receiving your deposit, Alloco begins the asset acquisition process.

Your order moves through several verification stages:

### 1. Subscription Submitted

Your mint request has been successfully created.

### 2. USDC Received

Your deposit has been confirmed.

### 3. Ramp-off Processing

Funds are prepared for execution.

### 4. Bank Confirmed

Settlement infrastructure confirms the available capital.

### 5. Broker Buying Power Confirmed

Execution capacity is verified with the connected broker.

### 6. Basket Executing

The underlying assets of the ETP are purchased according to the predefined strategy and allocation rules.

### 7. Claimable

Once the basket is fully executed and NAV is calculated, your ETP tokens are ready to claim.

---

# Step 3 — Claim Your ETP Tokens

### Receive your onchain investment product

After execution is completed, Alloco calculates the final execution NAV and determines the number of ETP units issued.

You will receive:

- Final execution NAV
- Total minted units
- Deposit amount
- Fees
- Settlement timestamp

Once confirmed, your ETP tokens can be claimed directly to your wallet.

Your tokens represent ownership of the corresponding ETP structure and its underlying assets.
