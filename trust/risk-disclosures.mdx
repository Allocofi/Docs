All investments involve risk, and Alloco ETP products are no exception. Before accessing any product, read the relevant product-specific risk disclosures carefully. This page provides an overview of the primary risk categories that apply across Alloco products — but it is not a substitute for the full risk disclosure document associated with any individual product.

<Warning>
You may lose part or all of your capital. Historical returns, backtests, simulations, target returns, expected returns, strategy descriptions or market narratives do not indicate future results.
</Warning>

## 1. Market and Strategy Risk

The value of any Alloco product may change — including falling to zero — due to conditions in financial markets and the broader economy. Factors that can affect product value include:

- Movements in underlying assets, crypto markets, equities, interest rates, commodities, FX, and macroeconomic conditions
- Geopolitics, regulation, technology cycles, and shifts in risk appetite
- Strategy-specific risks, including:
  - Funding rate reversals and basis spread widening
  - Hedge failure or hedging instruments becoming unavailable
  - Liquidity disappearing during stress events
  - Model assumption or correlation breakdowns
  - Execution costs eroding strategy returns

## 2. NAV, Premium/Discount and Liquidity Risk

NAV is an important valuation reference, but it is not a guaranteed trading price. NAV may be distorted by:

- Delayed data, incorrect price sources, or stale prices
- Market-hour mismatch between the underlying asset and the product token
- Insufficient underlying liquidity or corporate-action delays
- Oracle failure or delayed onchain updates

Product tokens may trade above or below NAV in secondary markets. Even where a create/redeem mechanism exists, the market price is not guaranteed to remain close to NAV at any given time.

You may also be unable to buy, sell, or redeem at your expected price or within your expected timeframe due to low DEX depth, AMM slippage, missing RFQ quotes, insufficient market-maker inventory, small AUM, or stress-market liquidity loss.

## 3. Redemption Risk

Redemption from Alloco ETP products is subject to product-specific terms, cycles, and constraints. The following scenarios may affect your redemption experience:

| Risk Scenario | Possible Impact |
|---|---|
| **Extended redemption cycle** | You may not receive assets within the expected time frame |
| **Execution-adjusted NAV** | The value redeemed may be lower than the reference NAV at the time you submitted your request |
| **Insufficient cash buffer** | Large redemptions may be queued or settled in batches rather than immediately |
| **Emergency Mode** | The system may suspend large creation, redemption or certain trading routes |

<Note>
Redemption may use T+1, T+N, weekly settlement or strategy-specific epochs. It may be affected by market closure, counterparty settlement delays, execution costs, risk-state pauses, fees and regulatory constraints.
</Note>

## 4. Crypto and TradFi-Linked Product Risk

Alloco products may reference both crypto-native markets and traditional financial assets. Each domain carries a distinct set of structural risks.

**Crypto-native risks:**

High volatility, exchange bankruptcy or freeze, withdrawal limits, stablecoin depeg, smart contract exploits, bridge risk, oracle manipulation, liquidation risk, MEV, gas spikes, governance attack, hacking, and private-key risk.

**TradFi-linked risks:**

Market-hour limits, weekend/holiday stale pricing, broker API failure, custody-account restrictions, corporate actions, trading halts, settlement mismatch, cross-border capital limits, tax and withholding issues, and synthetic exposure counterparty risk.

## 5. Counterparty, Custody and Technology Risk

Products may rely on a range of third parties, each of which introduces risk. Failure, default, bankruptcy, operational error, or regulatory restriction affecting any of these parties may impact product value, creation/redemption, liquidity, or your rights:

- **Service providers:** Issuers, custodians, brokers, exchanges, authorised participants, market makers, oracles, stablecoin issuers, and administrators
- **Technical infrastructure:** Smart contract vulnerabilities, front-end errors, API failure, oracle update failure, database errors, chain congestion, gas price anomalies, hacking, DDoS attacks, and third-party service interruptions

Custody risks include private-key leakage, multisig error, custodian bankruptcy, account freeze, asset commingling, record error, redemption-priority disputes, unclear legal title, asset-recovery difficulty, and cross-border enforcement limits.

## 6. Execution, Legal, Tax and Information Risk

**Execution risk:** Order execution may suffer from slippage, partial fill, failure, delay, wrong routing, expired RFQ quotes, front-running, or state mismatch between offchain and onchain systems.

**Legal and regulatory risk:** Products may be affected by securities, commodities, fund, derivatives, stablecoin, AML, sanctions, tax, cross-border offering, suitability, data-protection, and consumer-protection rules. Regulatory changes may restrict, suspend, delist, liquidate, or limit access to products.

**Tax risk:** Tax treatment of product tokens, redemptions, income, and transfers may vary by jurisdiction and investor classification. You are responsible for determining your own tax obligations.

**Information risk:** Disclosures may be delayed, incorrect, incomplete, inconsistent across sources, affected by translation differences, or not comparable across historical periods.

## 7. Model, Concentration and User Responsibility

Products may rely on models, parameters, rules, or algorithms. Risks include overfitting, distorted backtests, parameter failure, uncovered extreme events, broken correlations, incorrect liquidity assumptions, market-structure changes, and insufficient data samples.

Some products may be concentrated in a small number of assets, industries, countries, themes, or strategies. Concentration may amplify returns but also amplify losses.

You are responsible for understanding products, reading documents, judging risks, confirming eligibility, managing wallets and private keys, complying with applicable laws, handling your own taxes, evaluating trading prices, and bearing investment outcomes.

## 8. No Guaranteed Return

<Warning>
Product names containing terms such as "Stable," "Yield," "Hedge," "Arbitrage," "Income," "Protection" or similar terms do not imply principal protection, guaranteed yield, no risk or fixed return. Neutral strategies may lose money, arbitrage strategies may fail.
</Warning>

You should not treat any Alloco product as a deposit, principal-protected product, fixed-income instrument, risk-free arbitrage product, or stable-return product unless the product documents explicitly and unconditionally state otherwise.
