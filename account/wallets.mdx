ETP Launcher is an infrastructure layer that enables creators, asset managers, and institutions to design and launch their own onchain Exchange-Traded Products (ETPs).

By combining traditional investment structures with blockchain-native infrastructure, ETP Launcher transforms investment ideas into transparent, programmable, and accessible financial products.

Creators can define:

- Investment themes
- Underlying assets
- Portfolio allocation rules
- Rebalancing methodology
- Risk parameters
- Fee structures

The platform provides the infrastructure required for:

- Product structuring
- NAV calculation
- Token issuance
- Liquidity management
- Transparency reporting

Launching a traditional financial product requires significant infrastructure:

- Legal structuring
- Custody arrangements
- Fund administration
- NAV calculation systems
- Distribution channels
- Liquidity solutions

ETP Launcher abstracts these complexities into a standardized framework.

Creators can focus on investment ideas while the infrastructure layer handles product execution.
