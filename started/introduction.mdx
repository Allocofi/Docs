Alloco is financial product issuance and access infrastructure built for internet-native markets. The platform converts market narratives, professional insights, investment themes, and risk exposures into On-chain ETFT products with clear rules, transparent valuation, onchain tradability, and full risk disclosure — giving investors around the world access to Wall Street hedge funds, leading market voices, congressional trading disclosures, and global investment themes.

## What is On-chain ETFTs?

Imagine you believe the next wave of AI will be powered by data centers, energy, semiconductors and cooling infrastructure.

To invest in that idea, you would normally need to research multiple assets, open the right accounts, manage each position and rebalance the portfolio yourself.

ETFTs made this easier by packaging a basket of assets into one tradable product.

An On-chain ETFT brings the same idea onchain.

**It packages a portfolio, strategy or investment theme into a single tokenized product, with defined rules and a transparent net asset value.**

Instead of managing every asset individually, you can access the entire idea through one product.

**Think of it as an ETF, built for onchain markets.**
