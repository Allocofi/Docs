Every Alloco ETP product comes with a set of product documents that define the terms, risks, and structure of the investment. These documents are the authoritative reference for how a product works, what it costs, who can access it, and what risks it carries. Reading them before accessing any product is your responsibility as an investor.

## What Each Document Contains

<Expandable title="Prospectus">
  The prospectus defines the fundamental terms of the product. It contains:

  - Product name, ticker, and investment objective
  - Eligible assets and investment universe
  - Fee schedule and full cost structure
  - Subscription and redemption terms, including eligible routes and settlement cycles
  - Eligibility and investor classification requirements
  - Jurisdictional restrictions and offering limitations
  - Legal wrapper structure (SPV, SPC, or other applicable vehicle)
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Risk Disclosures">
  The risk disclosures document details every material risk category associated with the product. It contains:

  - Market and strategy-specific risks
  - NAV, liquidity, and redemption risks
  - Counterparty and custody risks
  - Technology and smart contract risks
  - Legal, tax, and regulatory risks
  - No-guaranteed-return disclaimer
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="NAV Methodology">
  The NAV methodology document explains exactly how the product's value is calculated. It contains:

  - NAV calculation formula and approach
  - Three-layer NAV system (Official NAV, iNAV, Risk NAV)
  - Data sources and validation rules
  - Haircut model and risk adjustments
  - NAV state machine and exception handling
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Custody Overview">
  The custody overview explains how the product's underlying assets are held and protected. It contains:

  - Asset holding structure (onchain and/or offchain)
  - SPV/SPC or asset pool arrangements
  - Third-party custodians and brokers
  - Reconciliation and audit procedures
  - Permissions and operational controls
</Expandable>

## Before You Invest

Follow these steps before subscribing to or purchasing any Alloco ETP product.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the product page">
    Go to [app.allocofi.com](https://app.allocofi.com) and locate the product you are considering.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download and read the full product documents">
    Access the Prospectus, Risk Disclosures, NAV Methodology, and Custody Overview linked from the product page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the risk disclosures carefully">
    Understand what you could lose, under what conditions, and what product-specific risks apply to your situation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check eligibility and jurisdictional restrictions">
    Confirm that you meet the investor classification requirements and that the product is available in your jurisdiction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Obtain independent professional advice if needed">
    If you are uncertain about whether a product is appropriate for you, consult an independent financial, legal, or tax adviser before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Proceed only if the product suits your needs">
    Subscribe or purchase only after you have read all documents, understand the terms and risks, and have determined that the product is consistent with your investment objectives and risk tolerance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Information shown on the Alloco platform is a summary and may not contain every term, risk, restriction, fee, or legal consequence associated with a product. The Product Documents control in all cases.
</Warning>

<Note>
If Product Documents conflict with information on the Alloco platform, the Product Documents will control with respect to the applicable product, investment, subscription, redemption, or ETP Unit.
</Note>
