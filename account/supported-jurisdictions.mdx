Access to Alloco products depends on where you are located. Not all products are available in every region, and certain jurisdictions may be restricted entirely based on regulatory requirements, sanctions compliance, or other legal considerations. These restrictions apply at both the platform level and the individual product level.

## How Geographic Restrictions Work

Alloco enforces geographic restrictions through a combination of automated and manual controls. These include:

- **IP geolocation** — your approximate location is detected from your IP address when you access the platform.
- **Identity verification and KYC data** — the jurisdiction and residency information you submit during onboarding is used to determine product eligibility.
- **Wallet screening** — your connected wallet address is checked against sanctions lists and risk databases.
- **Blockchain analytics** — on-chain activity associated with your wallet may be analysed to identify geographic risk indicators.
- **Self-certification during onboarding** — you will be asked to confirm your jurisdiction, residency status, and eligibility as part of the account setup process.

Alloco may update or tighten these controls at any time where reasonably necessary for legal, compliance, risk, or security reasons.

## Who Can Use Alloco

To access the Services, you must meet all of the following conditions:

- You are located in a jurisdiction where your use of the platform is lawful under applicable financial services, securities, and investment regulations.
- You are not subject to applicable sanctions or asset-freeze restrictions imposed by any jurisdiction.
- You are not acting on behalf of a person who would be prohibited from using the Services.
- You are in compliance with all local financial services, securities, AML, and tax laws that apply to your use of the platform.

## Product-Level Restrictions

Individual ETP products may carry additional jurisdiction restrictions that go beyond the platform-level rules. A product may be limited to specific investor types (such as accredited, professional, or qualified investors), particular countries, or persons who meet additional regulatory criteria.

Always review the Product Documents and any eligibility disclosures for a specific ETP before attempting to subscribe or acquire Units. Access to the platform does not mean that every product displayed is available to you.

<Note>
  A person may be permitted to access informational portions of the Services — such as browsing product descriptions or educational content — while remaining ineligible to acquire or redeem an ETP Unit. Browsing product information does not imply that a product is available to you or constitutes an offer.
</Note>

## Questions About Your Jurisdiction

Regulatory requirements vary significantly across jurisdictions and change over time. If you are unsure whether Alloco is available in your jurisdiction, or whether a specific product is accessible to you, contact [support@allocofi.com](mailto:support@allocofi.com) before attempting to access or transact in any product.

Do not rely solely on your ability to load the website or pass an initial screen as confirmation that a product is available to you.
