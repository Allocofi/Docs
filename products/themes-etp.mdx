Themes ETPs let you invest in curated baskets built around major long-term structural trends. Instead of picking individual assets and trying to build your own exposure, you gain access to a theme — like AI infrastructure, energy transition, or global macro cycles — through a structured, transparent onchain product. Each Themes ETP has clear basket construction rules, a defined rebalancing schedule, transparent NAV, and full disclosure of the underlying holdings.

## Available Themes

Alloco's Themes ETPs cover a wide range of macro and industry narratives. Current and planned thematic exposures include:

- **AI power and compute infrastructure** — exposure to the hardware, energy, and software stack powering AI development
- **Energy and grid modernization** — companies and assets tied to the transition and upgrade of global energy infrastructure
- **Healthcare and longevity** — biotech, pharma, and longevity-focused assets riding long-term demographic trends
- **Hard assets and commodity exposure** — gold, commodities, and inflation-linked assets for portfolio resilience
- **Urban growth and real estate-linked themes** — exposure to urbanisation, housing demand, and real estate infrastructure
- **Supply chains and logistics** — assets tied to global trade infrastructure, shipping, and distribution networks
- **Defense and security** — companies and themes linked to global defense spending and cybersecurity
- **Macro cycles and inflation themes** — broad exposure to macro regimes, rate cycles, and inflation dynamics

## How Themes Become Products

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify the macro trend">
    A macro trend or industry narrative is identified — either by the Alloco team, a partner institution, or a user with a market thesis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build the ETP blueprint">
    The Theme To Product Engine converts the thesis into a structured ETP blueprint, defining the eligible assets, basket construction rules, and rebalancing schedule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Issue the product">
    The product is issued with a clear NAV methodology, full disclosure documents, and a defined custody structure — ready for trading and subscription.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access and trade">
    Access the product through DEX, RFQ, or primary subscription depending on your eligibility and the product's distribution rules.
  </Step>
</Steps>
