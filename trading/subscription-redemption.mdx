Primary market subscription and redemption let eligible users create and redeem ETP Units directly at or near NAV, bypassing secondary market spreads. This process is typically used for larger positions and is subject to KYC, eligibility, and product-specific rules. Read through the steps below to understand what happens from the moment you submit a request to when your units — or your proceeds — are available.

## Subscription Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify eligibility">
    Confirm you meet all KYC, investor classification, and product-specific eligibility requirements before submitting a subscription request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review product documents">
    Read the prospectus, risk disclosures, NAV methodology, and subscription terms for the ETP you want to subscribe to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit a subscription request">
    Log in to [app.allocofi.com](https://app.allocofi.com) and submit your subscription request through the product page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transfer subscription assets">
    Send stablecoins, fiat currency, or another accepted asset as specified in the subscription terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compliance and reconciliation">
    Your funds are screened, reconciled, and made available to the relevant broker, custodian, or product account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Asset allocation">
    Portfolio assets are purchased or allocated according to the product's investment rules.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transaction confirmation">
    The transaction is confirmed and ETP Units become available for minting or claiming.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim your units">
    Complete any required claim transaction in your wallet to receive the ETP Units.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Completion times are estimates and not guaranteed. A transaction may be delayed, rejected, or cancelled due to compliance review, market closure, liquidity constraints, or other factors.
</Note>

## Redemption Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the product page">
    Go to [app.allocofi.com](https://app.allocofi.com) and open the ETP product page for the units you want to redeem.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit a redemption request">
    Specify the number of ETP Units you want to redeem and submit the request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete identity verification">
    Complete any required identity verification or wallet screening if prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Redemption value is determined">
    Your redemption value is calculated at a later valuation point, after execution and fee deductions — not at the price shown at the time of your request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Underlying assets are sold">
    The product's underlying assets are sold and proceeds are prepared for settlement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your proceeds">
    Complete any required withdrawal transaction in your wallet to receive your settlement proceeds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Settlement Timelines

Settlement timelines vary by product and strategy type. Check the product documents for the specific timeline that applies to your product.

| Settlement Type | Description |
|---|---|
| T+1 | Settlement the next business day |
| T+N | Multi-day settlement for complex strategies |
| Weekly | Weekly settlement cycle for strategy-based products |
| Epoch-based | Settlement tied to specific strategy cycles |

## Redemption Restrictions

Redemptions are not always immediate or unconditional. Depending on the product, your redemption request may be subject to:

- Minimum redemption amounts
- Cut-off times and dealing days
- Notice periods
- Redemption gates (limits on redemption volume per period)
- Lock-up periods
- Suspension during emergency states

<Warning>
  Submitting a redemption request does not guarantee immediate settlement at the NAV displayed when the request is submitted. The redemption value may be affected by fees, spreads, execution prices, market movements, and costs.
</Warning>

## Execution-Adjusted NAV

For large redemptions, the final settlement value may not equal the reference NAV shown at the time you submitted your request. Instead, it reflects the **execution-adjusted NAV** — the actual proceeds realised after liquidating the underlying assets, deducting trading fees, slippage, execution costs, and any applicable product fees. This mechanism protects existing holders from dilution caused by large redemptions in illiquid markets.
