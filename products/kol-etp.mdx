KOL ETPs give you access to the publicly disclosed positions, trading signals, and market insights of Key Opinion Leaders and leading market voices — structured into transparent, rules-based onchain products. Instead of trying to manually track or replicate a signal, you can gain direct, structured exposure through a product with defined NAV, clear custody arrangements, and accessible trading.

## What is a KOL ETP?

A KOL ETP is built around publicly disclosed positions, trading signals, and market insights from known market voices. Rather than relying on informal copy-trading or opaque signal services, each KOL ETP converts a specific set of signals or portfolio disclosures into a structured, rules-based product. You get transparent NAV, defined rebalancing schedules, custody disclosures, and redemption rights — all in a single investable wrapper.

The underlying signal may come from a public portfolio disclosure, a regularly published market view, or a tracked on-chain wallet. Whatever the source, the product rules define exactly how that signal maps to holdings, how often it rebalances, and how NAV is calculated.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Signal identification and validation">
    A market voice or portfolio signal is identified and validated against disclosure requirements and data availability standards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Blueprint creation">
    The Theme To Product Engine converts the signal into a structured ETP blueprint with defined eligible assets, rebalancing rules, and rebalancing schedules.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Product issuance">
    The product is issued with a clear NAV methodology, custody structure, and full risk disclosures — including prospectus, risk disclosure document, and custody overview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access and trading">
    You can access the product through DEX, RFQ, or primary subscription depending on your eligibility and the specific product rules.
  </Step>
</Steps>

- Based on publicly disclosed positions or validated market signals
- Weekly settlement / rebalance cycle
- Transparent holdings and NAV updated regularly
- Available through secondary market (DEX/RFQ) and primary create/redeem
- Full risk disclosures and product documents provided
